A return to oneself.

For fifteen years I have practiced across the disciplines of lifting, condition-specific lasers, threads, and stem cell therapy. The countless cases, the quiet hours of study, the small refinements that no shortcut can replace — these are the wealth I bring to every guest who walks through our doors.

At Roycel, our pursuit is not merely to turn back time, but to deliver an experience and a value that goes beyond it. The moment a person rediscovers their most luminous self — that is the moment we work for.

If we can give more people a richer quality of life, then this is work I will never stop doing. Roycel pursues healthy change, beyond healing.

Dr. Daewon Kim
