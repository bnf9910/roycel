Signature Treatment

Sofwave™
Lifting, redefined.

The cornerstone of the Roycel lifting protocol. Sofwave delivers high-intensity 11.5MHz ultrasound energy through 7 transducers to the precise dermal layer where wrinkles form — 1.5mm beneath the surface — without compromising surrounding tissue.

The result is a single-session treatment that lifts, smooths, and remodels — with no downtime, no incisions, and visible refinement that unfolds over the following weeks.

For deeper contour or skin-condition concerns, our physicians often layer Sofwave with Ulthera or Thermage — and pair it with our signature tear-trough filler for a complete refresh.
